# 🎯 Problem Statement: University Knowledge Base (UKB)

## 📌 Context
The University Knowledge Base is a RAG-powered (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) system designed to democratize access to university policy, circulars, and handbooks. While the technical foundation is robust, the success of the KB depends on high-quality data curation and active user engagement.

## ⚠️ The Problem
University documentation is often siloed across various department portals, frequently outdated, and written in dense bureaucratic language. This leads to:
1.  **Administrative Fatigue**: Staff answering the same repetitive queries.
2.  **Student Frustration**: Wasted time searching for crucial exam or fee information.
3.  **Data Inconsistency**: Conflict between old circulars and new handbooks.

## 🚀 Sharpened Objectives

### 1. Measurable Engagement Targets
We shift from "availability" to "active utility":
- **Query Resolution Rate**: 75% of student queries must be resolved within the app (no "Escalate to Admin" clicks) by end of Year 1.
- **User Adoption**: Reach 5,000 monthly active users (MAU) across the university.
- **Contributor Growth**: Maintain a pipeline of 20+ active student volunteers contributing new documents and verifying RAG responses.

### 2. Volunteer-Hour Accuracy Goals
Curation is human-in-the-loop. We measure the quality of volunteer labor:
- **Metadata Precision**: 98% accuracy in automated tagging (Topic, Year, Department) after one human volunteer review.
- **Conflict Resolution**: 100% of LLM-detected contradictions (Conflicts) must be flagged and resolved by a volunteer within 48 hours.
- **Time-to-Value**: A new circular must be ingested, chunked, and verified in under 15 minutes of cumulative volunteer effort.

### 3. Analytics: Retention & Participation
To ensure sustainability, we track the "Contributor Lifecycle":
- **Volunteer Retention**: Maintain >80% semester-over-semester retention of student contributors.
- **Departmental Parity**: Ensure at least 3 active contributors per major department (CSE, EEE, MBA, etc.) to prevent knowledge silos.
- **Contribution Depth**: Track "Verified Chunks per Volunteer Hour" to reward high-impact participants.

---

> [!IMPORTANT]
> These metrics will be integrated into the Admin Dashboard to provide real-time visibility into the health of the Knowledge Base.
